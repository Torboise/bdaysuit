How to survive a Logan attack

Should you come across a Logan this example speech guide should help you out

  1. Logan: Makes a disrespectful comment.
    • Your response: "Logan, that comment is inappropriate and disrespectful. Let's keep the conversation respectful."
  2. Logan: Interrupts and talks over others.
    • Your response: "Logan, that comment is inappropriate and disrespectful. Let's keep the conversation respectful."
  3. Logan: Mocks or belittles someone.
    • Your response: "Logan, we should treat everyone with respect. Mocking or belittling others is not acceptable behavior."
  4. Logan: Engages in excessive bragging or boasting.
    • Your response: "Logan, it's great to have confidence, but let's focus on the topic at hand without excessive self-promotion."
  5. Logan: Uses offensive language or slurs.
    • Your response: "Logan, we must maintain a respectful and inclusive conversation. Offensive language is not welcome here."
  6. Logan: Acts aggressively or confrontationally.
    • Your response: "Logan, there's no need for aggression. We can discuss this calmly and constructively."
  7. Logan: Drinks excessively or pressures others to do so.
    • Your response: "Logan, it's important to drink responsibly and respect others' choices. Let's have a good time without pressuring anyone."
  8. Logan: Disregards rules or safety.
    • Your response: "Logan, safety and rules are in place for a reason. Let's respect them to ensure everyone's well-being."
  9. Logan: Engages in hazing or harmful behavior.
    • Your response: "Logan, hazing and harmful behavior are not acceptable. Let's promote a positive and supportive environment."
